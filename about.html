About COMPUTOR: A COMPUTER ENGINEERING FUNDAMENTALS TUTOR

In today’s generation, it is foremost observable and seen that technology and the internet are becoming a necessity in every single person’s daily living. The vast majority of the activities done by each person every single day almost involves the use of the internet. The internet is used in many different ways like communication, research, entertainment, work, study, and etc. While technology on the other side, eases the way of doing things. The advances that technology provided made significant effects on human life. Taking all that in consideration, the effective use of both the internet and technology can have a big impact on humans.

Computers, laptops and mobile phones are some of the technologies used in daily lives of a person. Those gadgets are a big help in making the life of a person easy. Those specific tools can be a means of accessing the internet. Also it can be used to do school work and research.

Computor is a mobile-friendly website that provides a free computer engineering tutorial using different videos that came from Youtube. This is a site in which you can learn and test your understanding about different subjects.
